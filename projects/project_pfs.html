Future Release of the P.F.S. Project

Scheduled day for the release of the Beta version of my project "Personal Finance System", an app for the managment of investments and of savings that aims at creating one single venue to track all your balances at once.

The app "Personal Finance System" is my personal project to simplify the process of managing different files aimed at tracking transactions, bank account balance and potential investment balance.
Thanks to it, you can keep all of this inside a single app, with a simple UI, and user-friendly and guided interactions. The app also shows your balance history, your investments progress and more, in the form of a graph that shows a balance aganist time for an easy and intuitive result.

Some features of the App:

  • Easy to insert templates for adding the details necessary for the recordings
  • Graphs that show the histoy of your balances and the progress of your investments
  • Build-in Compound interest calculator with an optimizer for time of the compound
  • All data are hashed and protected by a password for your own privacy
  • All data are local, nothing is saved outside of your personal space
